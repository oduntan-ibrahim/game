Rock Paper Scissors

Rules of the game.

  1. Rock beats Scissors but Lose to Paper.
  2. Paper beats Rock but Lose to Scissors.
  3. Scissors beats Paper but Lose to Rock.

How to play ?

  1. You can pick from the Rock, paper and Scissors button Above.
  2. You can use the AutoPlay button to let computer pick random moves for you and press the Stop play to stop computer from picking moves for you.
  3. You can also use your keyboard if you are using a PC.
    • 'R' for Rock
    • 'P' for Paper
    • 'S' for Scissors
    • 'A' for AutoPlay
